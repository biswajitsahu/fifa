The birthday paradox states that in any group of 26 people there is a 50% chance that two of them share a birthday.
The World Cup 2022 in Qatar had 32 teams with a squad of 26 players each.
Scrape this Wikipedia page https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2022_FIFA_World_Cup_squads
for the birthdays of players and find how many teams out of the 32 have at least 2 players who share the same birthday.
We're looking for two players within the same team who have the same birthday, the total number of teams where there is at least one such pair of players.
